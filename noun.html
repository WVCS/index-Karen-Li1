Noun Project: Day6

Introduction

Day6 is a South-Korean band formed under the label JYP Entertainment. The group debuted on September 7, 2015 with the EP The Day . The band released two songs every month in 2017 as part of their project, Every Day6. Their discography contains a range of genres including pop and rock. Their first full-length album, Sunrise, consists of the first half of the songs from the Every Day6 project.

Members

  • Jae Park (Park Jaehyun) - guitar, vocals, rap
  • Park Sungjin - leader, main vocals, guitar
  • Young K (Kang Younghyun) - vocals, rap, bass
  • Kim Wonpil - keyboard, synthesizer, vocals
  • Yoon Dowoon - drums, vocals

Even of Day

Even of Day is a subunit of Day6 consisting of members Young K, Wonpil, and Dowoon. They debuted on August 31, 2020 with the EP The Book of Us: Gluon - Nothing can tear us apart.
