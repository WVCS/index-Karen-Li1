DAY6

Introduction

Day6 is a South-Korean band formed under the label JYP Entertainment. The group debuted on September 7, 2015 with the EP The Day . The band released two songs every month in 2017 as part of their project, Every Day6. Their discography contains songs in genres ranging from rock, mellow, and upbeat songs. Their first full-length album, Sunrise, contained the first half of the songs from the Every Day6 project.

Members

  • Jae Park (Park Jaehyun) - guitar, vocals, rap
  • Park Sungjin - leader, main vocals, guitar
  • Young K (Kang Younghyun) - vocals, rap, bass
  • Kim Wonpil - keyboard, synthesizer, vocals
  • Yoon Dowoon - drums, vocals
