Convince Project: Birds Aren't Real

Why?

An online conspiracy movement claims that the government replaced birds with surveillance drones after killing 12 million birds with a virus from 1959-2001. The movement states that the reasoning behind killing the birds was because directors in the CIA were annoyed at birds for pooping on their cars. Birds are also ideal for surveillance as their ability to fly allowed a better view of the target, thus becoming the much more convenient solution for stealing information and tracking citizens.

Non-Believers

People who disagree with the theory claim that those who believe that birds aren't real are merely upholding the amusing parody conspiracy and that there is innumerable evidence that supports the assertion that birds are in fact real.
