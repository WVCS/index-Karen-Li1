PROFILE

The Rose

The Rose (더 로즈) is a South Korean band formed by J&Star Company. The band is composed of four members: Kim Woo-sung, Park Do-joon, Lee Ha-joon, and Lee Jae-hyeong. Prior to its debut with J&Star, the band had long been a popular indie group named Windfall before making its official mainstream release with "Sorry" on August 3, 2017. The group then made a subsequent comeback on October 31, 2017 with its second single, "Like We Used To." In 2018, the group was listed as one of the top 5 Korean breakout artists to watch in 2018 by Billboard and won Most Promising Artist at the International K-Music Awards, in addition to being nominated as Rookie of the Year for the 13th Annual Soompi Awards. On April 16, 2018, The Rose released their first mini-album entitled Void and later that year on October 4, they released their second mini-album entitled Dawn. On August 13, 2019, the band released their third single album Red. They released the single "Black Rose" on August 23, 2020 as an independent artist after the band requested the termination of their contract with their J&Star, citing unreasonable schedules and problems with payments. The band has been on hiatus after Dojoon, Hajoon, and Jaehyeong enlisted for their mandatory military service the same year.

WOOSUNG

Kim Woo-sung (김우성) was born on February 25, 1993 and is the lead vocalist and guitarist for The Rose. He made his solo debut on July 25, 2019 with the his EP Wolf, releasing "Lonely" and "Beautiful Girl" the same year. He is also performs on the Catch The Ghost, Psychopath Diary and Itaewon Class OSTs.

DOJOON

Park Do-joon (박도준) was born on April 20, 1993 and is the main vocalist, keyboardist and acoustic guitarist for The Rose. He appears in Eddy Kim's "Coffee & Tea" music video with Hajoon and Jaehyeong. He is currently completing his military service.

HAJOON

Lee Ha-joon (이하준) was born on July 29. 1994 and is the drummer, sub-vocalist and rapper for the The Rose. He appears in Eddy Kim's "Coffe & Tea" music video with Dojoon and Jaehyeong. He is currently completing his military service.

JAEHYEONG

Lee Jae-hyeong (이재형) was born on November 3, 1994 and is the bassist and sub-vocalist for The Rose. He also appears in Eddy Kim's "Coffee & Tea" music video with Dojoon and Hajoon. He is currently completing his military service.
